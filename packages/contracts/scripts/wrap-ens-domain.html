🔐 Wrap ENS Domain

Wrap support-dev.eth with CANNOT_UNWRAP fuse for ForeverSubnameRegistrar

Why this is needed:

The ForeverSubnameRegistrar creates subdomains with PARENT_CANNOT_CONTROL fuse. To allow this, the parent domain must have CANNOT_UNWRAP fuse burned.

1Connect Wallet

Connect your MetaMask wallet on Sepolia network.

2Approve NameWrapper

Allow NameWrapper to transfer your domain NFT from BaseRegistrar.

Contract: BaseRegistrar
to: 0x0635513f179D50A207757E05759CbD106d7dFcE8
tokenId: 0xbf7423a8dd10b7a51c13dedd43d0b64e52728e01bf8c0d327f2ab185a75f9262

✅ Approval successful! Transaction:

3Wrap Domain

Wrap support-dev.eth with CANNOT_UNWRAP fuse (value: 1).

Contract: NameWrapper
label: "support-dev"
wrappedOwner: 0xf66CcEDcD3f99C234cefA713Ab7399F5DD3a6770
ownerControlledFuses: 1 (CANNOT_UNWRAP)
resolver: 0xE99638b40E4Fff0129D56f03b55b6bbC4BBE49b5
